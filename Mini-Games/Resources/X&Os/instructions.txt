X & Os / Noughts and Crosses/ Tic Tac Toe, 
is a two-player game where players take turns placing 
either an "X" or an "O" on a 3x3 grid. 
The objective of the game is to get three of your symbols 
in a row (either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) 
before your opponent does. If all spaces on the board are 
filled and neither player has achieved this goal, 
the game is a tie.
To play chose a number from the displayed board, between 1-9.
Where each number corresponds to a section on the board.
